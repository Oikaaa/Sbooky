Details

Director: Fede Álvarez

Genre: Horror, Thriller, Slasher, Crime film

Release dates: March 12, 201

Running time: 88 minutes

Rate: Rated R for terror, violence, disturbing content, and language including sexual references. Violence: - Frequent explicit violence. - Portrayal of sexual violence, with no nudity or detail.

Description

Rocky, Alex, and Money are three Detroit delinquents who make a living by breaking into houses and stealing valuables. Rocky longs to move to California with her younger sister, Diddy, and escape from their abusive mother and her alcoholic boyfriend.
